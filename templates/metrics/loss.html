Model Loss History (MAE)

Training vs. Validation Loss

This graph tracks the Mean Absolute Error (MAE) loss over 100 training epochs. Loss measures the difference between the model's prediction and the actual value; therefore, a lower value is better.

  • Blue Line (Training Loss): Shows the error on the training data. The sharp initial drop indicates rapid learning.
  • Orange Line (Validation Loss): Shows the error on unseen validation data. It stabilizes around 0.01.

Conclusion: The validation loss tracks closely with the training loss and does not spike upwards, which confirms that the model is robust and generalized, with no significant overfitting.
