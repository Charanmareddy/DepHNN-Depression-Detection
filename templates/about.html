At DepHNN, our mission is to advance the integration of artificial intelligence in the field of mental health by developing reliable and data-driven tools for the early detection of depression.

Through the analysis of EEG (Electroencephalogram) signals, we aim to identify neural patterns that may indicate depressive disorders. Our goal is to provide clinicians and researchers with a supportive, AI-powered solution that enables faster, more objective, and evidence-based screening for mental health conditions.

We are deeply committed to fostering innovation in healthcare while ensuring that technology is applied ethically, responsibly, and empathetically‚Äîalways keeping the well-being of individuals at the center of our mission.

üíª The Technology

DepHNN employs a hybrid neural network model that combines the strengths of multiple deep learning architectures to analyze EEG data with exceptional precision.

The system is designed to capture both spatial and temporal features from brainwave patterns, thereby enhancing the accuracy and reliability of depression classification.

Our web-based platform empowers users to seamlessly upload EEG recordings, visualize analytical results, and receive AI-driven insights in real time, providing a user-friendly experience for both clinicians and researchers.

Built using Python, TensorFlow, and Django, DepHNN ensures a high standard of scalability, security, and computational efficiency, making it suitable for both research and clinical applications.
